The Process

Five stages, in this order, for a reason.

Every STRATA system — home or industrial — runs the same sequence. Skipping a stage or reordering it is the most common reason a purifier fails early.

01

Sediment Filter

A 5-micron spun filter strips sand, silt and rust particles before they can scratch or clog anything downstream.

02

Activated Carbon

Removes chlorine, pesticide residue and the odours that make borewell or municipal water taste "off."

03

RO Membrane

A semi-permeable membrane down to 0.0001 micron rejects dissolved salts, heavy metals and fluoride — the step that actually lowers TDS.

04

UV Sterilization

Ultraviolet light neutralizes bacteria and viruses that survive filtration — essential for open-well or tanker-fed supply.

05

Mineral Cartridge

Reintroduces calcium and magnesium the RO stage strips out, so the water tastes natural instead of flat.
